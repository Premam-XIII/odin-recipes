Pizza

Description

A classic Margherita pizza with a crispy base, tomato sauce, and melted mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough base
  • 3 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 100g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on the dough base.
  3. Add mozzarella on top.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
  5. Top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.
