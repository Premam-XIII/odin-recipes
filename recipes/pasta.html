Pasta

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish with a rich tomato and meat sauce. Simple to make and incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g ground beef
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until cooked.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan.
  3. Add garlic and tomatoes, simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Mix sauce with pasta and serve.
