Omelette

Description

A simple and quick French omelette — soft, fluffy, and perfect for any meal of the day.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, season with salt and pepper, and whisk well.
  2. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and stir gently.
  4. When nearly set, fold in half and slide onto a plate.
